## 2024-03-24 - [Sim Mode Output Cleanup]
**Learning:** Users perceive "process logs" (checklists of execution stages) as debugging noise, not analysis. When "System Analysis" is the goal, removing lifecycle visualizations improves clarity and trust.
**Action:** In future "Analysis" views, prioritize the *outcome* (observations/interpretation) over the *process* (execution steps), and ensure status elements have ARIA labels.
